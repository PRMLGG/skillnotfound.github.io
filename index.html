Loot & Trial Process

Transparent systems designed to reward performance and ensure fair distribution.

üí∞ Bind on Equip (BoE) Items

Early raid BoEs are often Bind on Pickup and will be distributed via loot council like normal items. Once items become BoE, all BoEs will traded to the guild to be sold to fund guild consumables and repairs.

Currently, 100% of BoE sales go to the guild bank (subject to change as needed).

üé≤ Loot Distribution

Loot is distributed fairly with a focus on:

  • DPS - Prioritized for efficient early kills
  • Healers - Secondary priority
  • Tanks - Tertiary priority

Gear is more quickly acquired through Mythic+ and the Great Vault, so this priority helps maximize raid progression speed.
